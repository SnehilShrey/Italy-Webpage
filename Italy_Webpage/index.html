Italy – A Land of Beauty and History

Italy is my favorite country because of its rich history, amazing food, and breathtaking cities. Two cities that I find the most fascinating are Rome and Venice.

Rome – The Eternal City

Rome is the capital of Italy and known for its ancient architecture like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City.

Venice – The Floating City

Venice is famous for its canals, romantic gondola rides, and unique charm. The city is built on water and has no roads—only canals and bridges!
